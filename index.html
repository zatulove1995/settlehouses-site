What we do

We take on the homes the market leaves behind.

Many real estate funds compete with individual buyers for move-in-ready homes. We take a different approach — focusing on distressed properties that traditional banks won't finance and retail buyers can't take on.

What we buy

Single-family homes from $60k–$300k that need renovation. Because most banks won't finance distressed inventory, we're not competing with retail buyers.

Where we buy

We focus on Sun Belt markets — Georgia, Texas, Alabama, and other high-growth cities where affordable housing is needed most.

How we move

We buy in any condition and can close in as little as two weeks. Most purchases are made sight-unseen when the photos tell us enough.

Our mission

Quality, affordable housing for families across America.

By transforming homes that would otherwise sit vacant, we reduce neighborhood blight and return them to use as high-quality, affordable rentals.

Every house we renovate is one more family that gets a place they can proudly call home — and one more block on its way back.
